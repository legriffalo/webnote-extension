The page you're opening the reader on is sadly incompatable, it is most likely a browser settings page or another area of your browser that does not have any web-based content.

Visit a site such as youtube to use our extension

controls/features

  • Hide: Minify the control panel
  • Share button:Push your markups to a database and generate a share code
  • Width input: Locks the screen width to ensure shared mark ups read the same for evryone
  • Layers: Create mark up layers to break down complex info, or accomodate numerous screen widths/breakpoints
  • Save : Update your shared mark ups on the db
